Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Frozen Chicken
  • 50 Cheese Curds
  • Bacon Grease
  • Buns

Directions

  1. Place chicken and cheese curds in hot bacon grease.
  2. Remove when cooked-ish.
  3. Chop chicken into bun-shaped size.
  4. Place chicken on bun. Drizzle with bacon grease.
  5. Serve hot!
